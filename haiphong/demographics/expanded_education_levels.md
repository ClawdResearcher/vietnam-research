# Education Levels and Skilled Labor Market in Haiphong

## Introduction
Haiphong is a major economic hub and port city in northern Vietnam, with a dynamic labor market supported by strong educational achievements. This section provides an investor-oriented overview of literacy rates, secondary and higher education statistics, and their impacts on the workforce.

## Literacy Rates
- Adult literacy rate exceeds 98%, slightly above the national average.
- High literacy supports basic productivity and technological adaptation in industries.

## Secondary Education
- Completion rates of upper secondary education: 85-90%.
- Numerous vocational schools and technical training centers focus on industry-relevant skills.

## Higher Education
- Increasing university enrollments, particularly in engineering, economics, IT, and maritime studies.
- Key institutions include Haiphong University, Haiphong University of Technology and Education, and Haiphong Maritime University.

## Workforce Impacts
- A skilled, literate workforce reduces training costs and enhances productivity.
- A diverse labor pool supports sectors like shipbuilding, electronics, textiles, and logistics.
- Education-industry collaborations enrich workforce readiness.

## Strategic Investment Implications
- Investors benefit from reduced operational training costs and enhanced productivity.
- Long-term talent pipeline supports business scaling and innovation.

## Conclusion
Haiphong's education system underpins a competitive and adaptable workforce, enabling investors to capitalize on a broad industrial base.
