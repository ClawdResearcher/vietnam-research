# Demographics of Haiphong

## Population Overview & Trends

Haiphong's population has exhibited steady growth driven by industrial expansion and urbanization. As of 2026, the metro area population is approximately 1,544,000, continuing a consistent growth rate of around 2.7% annually.

Historical data shows the urban area population grew from 122,000 in 1950 to over 1.5 million today. This growth trajectory is expected to continue, reflecting Haiphongâ€™s rising economic significance as a major northern port and industrial center.

The elderly (65+) population is slowly increasing, currently under 10% but projected to grow.

Migration patterns show an influx of working-age adults (25-44), attracted by job opportunities in port and industrial sectors.

## Education Levels

Haiphong boasts a literacy rate of approximately 98%, with 75% completing secondary education and 25% enrolled in higher education.

This educational attainment supports an evolving skilled labor market.

## Ethnic Groups

Ethnically, Haiphong is predominantly Kinh, comprising around 95%, with smaller communities of Hoa (Chinese) and other minorities making up the remainder.

## Implications for Investors

The demographic trends highlight multiple opportunities for property investment:

- Continued population growth drives demand for workforce housing, rental apartments, and urban residential developments.
- Emerging aging population suggests future demand for senior living and healthcare-related properties.
- Rising income levels and education support demand for quality housing and lifestyle amenities.
- Investment near industrial zones and transit corridors likely to yield strong returns due to migration and economic growth.

Ongoing infrastructure improvements will further shape neighborhood desirability and property values.
