# Population Overview & Trends in Haiphong

Haiphong, Vietnam's third-largest city, has experienced consistent population growth, reaching approximately 1,544,000 residents in the metro area by 2026. This reflects a steady annual growth rate of about 2.7% in recent years, indicative of strong urbanization and economic development.

| Year | Population (Metro Area) | Annual Growth Rate (%) |
|------|------------------------|-----------------------|
| 2022 | 1,423,000              | 2.97                  |
| 2023 | 1,464,000              | 2.88                  |
| 2024 | 1,504,000              | 2.73                  |
| 2025 | 1,544,000              | 2.66                  |
| 2026 | 1,544,000 (Projected)  | -                     |

The sustained population increase is mainly fueled by rural-to-urban migration and industrial growth, enhancing demand for housing, infrastructure, and public services. This demographic momentum positions Haiphong as a promising market for residential and commercial property investment.
